Understanding Student Loans

About

This website is designed to help you understand the student loan system in the UK. It is designed to be simple and easy to use. Each article is divided into 3 sections:

  • Understanding the application process
  • Understanding the loan system
  • Understanding the repayment system
Each section will have relevant information simplified and broken down to make it easier to understand. To begin, click on one of the tiles below to select your funding body.
